About Us

Pets Peeps is dedicated to providing the best quality care for your pet. We offer daily visits for your pet, which may include walking, feedings, play times, or just good company, depending on your needs. We strive to be the most dependable and accessible service across India and will go the extra distance to give you exactly what you're looking for in pet care.
